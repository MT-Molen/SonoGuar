The Problem

Knowledge Gap

Healthcare sustainability is a growing institutional and regulatory priority — yet no published study has ever quantified the environmental footprint of medical simulation phantoms. Commercial ultrasound phantoms are made from petroleum-derived polymers, manufactured industrially, shipped internationally, and discarded as plastic medical waste. SonoGuar is synthesized in 10 minutes from food-grade agricultural ingredients, produced on-site, and biodegrades on disposal. This comparison has never appeared in the literature. Being first is the entire advantage.

Central Hypothesis

"SonoGuar produces substantially lower greenhouse gas emissions, plastic waste, and petroleum-derived material use per training session compared to commercial ultrasound phantoms across all five life-cycle stages — and full U.S. adoption would eliminate tens of thousands of kilograms of plastic medical waste annually."

Methods

🔢6-Step Protocol

  • 1
    Define Scope & Functional Unit
    Functional unit: 1 ultrasound-guided procedure training session. System boundary: cradle-to-grave. Three impact categories: CO₂-eq, plastic waste, water use.
    Standard: ISO 14040/14044 LCA methodology
  • 2
    Build SonoGuar Life Cycle Inventory
    Itemize all ingredients and weights from Bikkani et al. 2026. Link to ecoinvent background processes for guar gum, glycerol, canola oil, borax. Negligible energy input.
    Source: Bikkani et al. supplementary + ecoinvent database
  • 3
    Build Commercial Phantom Inventory
    Estimate PVC/polyurethane composition from product specs and published LCA literature. Model shipping (air vs. sea). Amortize total impact over lifespan (300–1,000 sessions).
    Sources: ecoinvent · EPA WARM · published polymer LCA values
  • 4
    Run LCA in OpenLCA
    Free LCA software. Import ecoinvent database. Run CML 2002 or ReCiPe midpoint method. Output CO₂-eq, plastic waste, and water use per functional unit for both products.
    Tool: openlca.org (free) · EPA WARM (free shortcut)
  • 5
    Scale to National Impact
    Apply per-session savings to full U.S. adoption (700 programs × 40 sessions/yr). Convert to EPA equivalencies — trees, car miles — for public communication.
    Tool: EPA GHG Equivalencies Calculator (free)
  • 6
    Write — Lead With Sustainability
    Frame for the green healthcare audience, not the simulation audience. Open with healthcare's 4.4% GHG share. SonoGuar is the solution — not the subject.
    Reporting: ISO 14044 · ILCD Handbook

⚠Pitfalls & Fixes

  • Commercial phantom composition is unknown
    Fix: Use published LCA literature values for generic PVC (~6 kg CO₂/kg) and polyurethane (~4 kg CO₂/kg). Email manufacturer for MSDS. State assumption clearly — it's a limitation, not a flaw.
  • SonoGuar lifespan not formally tested under repeated use
    Fix: Cite self-healing data from Bikkani et al. (G' recovery, needle tract healing). Use 1-session lifespan as conservative base case. Make formal durability testing an explicit future work recommendation.
  • ecoinvent database requires institutional access
    Fix: Most university libraries provide access. If unavailable, use published per-kg CO₂ values from peer-reviewed LCA literature and the EPA WARM model for plastic waste — both free and citable.
  • Reviewers unfamiliar with LCA methodology
    Fix: Cite ISO 14044 compliance in Methods. Include a 2-sentence LCA primer in the introduction. Consider a supplementary file with the full inventory table to show transparency.
